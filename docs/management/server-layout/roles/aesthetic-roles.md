## Other Roles

### Birthday Role

On users' birthdays, they can request the 'Enjoy a MARVELous B-day!' role.

### Subreddit Mod

Subreddit mods are users who are also moderators of the [r/Marvel](https://reddit.com/r/Marvel) subreddit.
